1. Taints and tolerance are to schedule and restrict the pod to the nodes
Taint - Node 
Toleration - pods
" kubectl taint nodes <node-name> key=value:taint-effect "
- taint-effect - No-schedule - dont schedule the pod if the tolerate is not applied.
- taint-effect - PreferNoSchedule - this will max avoid the pod with the tolerate if not then it will accept.
- taint-effect - NoExecute - This will only restrict the new pods with the tolerate and not the similar pods which was already in the node with no tolerate applied.
2. pod-definition.yml
apiVersion: v1
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
kind: Pod
metadata: 
 name: mypod
 labels
    app: myapp
	type: front-end
spec:
  containers:
  - name: nginx-container
    image: nginx
	
  tolerations:
  - key: "app"
    operator: "Equal"
	value: "blue"
	effect: "NoSchedule"
